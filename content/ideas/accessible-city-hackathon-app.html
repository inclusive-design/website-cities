--- title: Accessible City Hackathon Application: OCADU + Sidewalk Labs date: "2018-09-07" ---

Throughout August and September, Sidewalk Labs partnered with the Inclusive Design Research Centre at the Ontario College of Art and Design to facilitate three co-design sessions to inform our accessibility principles and planning work. This was a fantastic opportunity to brainstorm, workshop and co-create ideas for potential accessible city systems with people who identify as part of the accessibility community in Toronto.

Now, we're continuing the work with a hackathon to start building prototypes and testing out how some of these ideas might work!

WHEN: Friday, September 21, 2018, 9am-7pm

WHERE: 307 Lakeshore Blvd E, Toronto, ON M5A 1C1, Canada https://goo.gl/maps/y4W7ruWqGmE2
